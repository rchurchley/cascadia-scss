Example Usage

Cascadia is a SASS theme to bootstrap your way to a simple mobile-friendly website. It features a fixed-height image header and a mobile-friendly menu for up to four links.

The Pacific Northwest, sometimes referred to as Cascadia, is a region in western North America characterized by its mountainous geography, liberal culture, and temperate climate. It includes the Canadian province of British Columbia, the US states of Oregon and Washington, and the watersheds of the rivers flowing to the Pacific through North America's temperate rainforests.

Usage

First, make sure your webpage follows the structure illustrated in TEMPLATE.html. Compile the main stylesheet with 

sass --load-path . main.scss
and add the resulting CSS to your project.

Customization

The color scheme (among other things) can be customized in _settings.scss. Any variables defined there will override the default options in partials/_base.scss.
